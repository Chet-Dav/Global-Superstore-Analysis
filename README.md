## Analysis:
### What sales trends exist across time?

Monthly sales show noticeable fluctuations, indicating seasonality rather than a steady pattern. There are small drops in February, April, and July, but sales increase strongly from August to December. December records the highest sales, showing that the last quarter of the year is the most profitable period for the business.

### Which products are top-performing and which are underperforming?

Technology products, especially smartphones, generate the highest revenue. Overall, the Technology category leads in sales, followed by Furniture. Office Supplies generates the lowest revenue among the three categories, making it the weakest performer.

### How does sales performance vary across regions?

Regional analysis shows that the Central region generates the highest revenue, particularly within the Consumer segment. North and North Asia also perform strongly, while Oceania and South contribute comparatively less. Across most regions, the Consumer segment drives the largest portion of sales, followed by Corporate and Home Office.

### Which regions contribute most to revenue and shipment frequency?

Shipping performance indicates Standard Class shipping generates the most revenue, far higher than other shipping options. This shows that customers prefer more affordable delivery methods. Regions with higher sales, especially Central and North, also record higher shipment activity due to more orders.
Customer performance analysis further reveals a very high repeat customer rate of 95.92 percent, indicating strong retention and customer loyalty. Customer growth remains positive overall, with peak activity recorded in November. Sales per customer also reflect strong value generation per client.

## Conclusion and Recommendation:
Overall, the analysis indicates strong revenue generation, healthy profitability, and exceptional customer retention. The business performs particularly well in the Technology category, the Central region, and during the final quarter of the year.

To strengthen performance further, the company should capitalize on Q4 demand through improved inventory planning and targeted marketing. Expanding efforts in high-performing regions and encouraging premium shipping adoption may also increase profitability. 

By refining regional strategies and optimizing product focus, the business can convert its strong sales momentum into sustained long-term growth.
